About

I am a master's student in Quantum Technology with a research focus in experimental condensed matter physics. My primary interests lie in low-dimensional systems, particularly two-dimensional materials and their electronic and quantum properties.

I am interested in understanding how quantum phenomena emerge in real materials and how they can be measured and explored through experiment.
